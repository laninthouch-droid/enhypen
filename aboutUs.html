About Us

ENHYPEN TYPE is a fan-run store built for ENGENEs, by ENGENEs. We started this project to make it easier for fans everywhere to find authentic photocards, albums, and lightsticks in one place without the stress of overpriced resellers or confusing group orders.

Our Mission

To give every ENGENE fair, easy access to ENHYPEN merchandise, no matter where in the world they're from.

Our Promise

Every photocard and album we list is 100% authentic and carefully checked before it ships.
